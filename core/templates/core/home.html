How it works

A clear flow for every role

Acadlink adapts to how each participant in the school ecosystem actually operates day-to-day.

Student flow
1 Sign in and review current assignments.
2 Log a daily mood check-in.
3 Submit or resubmit homework with files or text.
4 Track approval status and teacher feedback.
Teacher flow
1 Create assignments with clear deadlines.
2 Review pending submissions quickly.
3 Approve, give feedback, or request resubmission.
4 Monitor class workload and completion rates.
Parent flow
1 Link with your child's account after admin approval.
2 Set daily free-time availability.
3 Track homework load and mood trends.
4 Message teachers directly when support is needed.
